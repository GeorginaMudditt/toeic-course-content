Part 3: Discussion Questions

Use the vocabulary from this worksheet to answer the following questions.

  1. How do you prefer to communicate at work (e.g. talking on the phone, texting, writing emails)? Why?
  2. How often do you write emails? What makes a good professional email?
  3. Have you ever had to call a helpline or call centre? What was your experience?
  4. What is more effective for different demographics: a brochure or a website? Explain your opinion.
