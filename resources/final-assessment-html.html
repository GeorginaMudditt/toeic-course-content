Express an opinion

You will give your opinion about a specific topic. Be sure to say as much as you can in the time allowed. You will have 45 seconds to prepare. Then you will have 60 seconds to speak.

Topic: In the area where you live, would you prefer for the government to spend more money on improving public transportation systems, on maintaining green spaces, such as parks, or on something else? Give specific reasons or examples to support your opinion.

Reading

Incomplete Sentences

  1. New patients should arrive fifteen minutes before …………… scheduled appointments. ( themselves / their / them / they )
  2. Ms Choi offers clients …………… tax preparation services and financial management consultations. ( only if / either / both / not only )
  3. Because several committee members have been delayed, the accounting report will be discussed …………… than planned at today's meeting. ( late / latest / later / lateness )
  4. According to the revised schedule, the manufacturing conference will begin at 9:00 AM …………… 8:00 AM. ( now / when / due to / instead of )
  5. The restaurant critic for the Montreal Times …………… the food at Corban's Kitchen as affordable and authentic. ( ordered / admitted / described / purchased )
  6. …………… delays in the entryway construction, the conference centre will reopen and provide an alternative entrance until all work is complete. ( Furthermore / Assuming that / Regardless of / Subsequently )

Reading Comprehension

Memo

May 15

We would like to announce the upcoming retirement of Ken Esser. Mr Esser began his 30-year career here at The Terra Fund as a wildlife ranger in the California Wildlife Park. He has held seven different positions, eventually becoming the general director of conservation for all West Coast Wildlife Parks. He has been in this position for the past 15 years, leading with vision and commitment. Now at the age of 65, he is leaving us for a well-deserved retirement.

The board of directors has voted to give him a Lifetime Achievement Award and will present him with a commemorative plaque at the staff meeting next Friday. Following the staff meeting, we invite all employees to stay for a reception to honor Mr Esser and his great contributions. If you would like to write a farewell note to Mr Esser, please stop by Andrew Braun's office to sign a book that will be presented at the reception.

  1. In what field does Mr Esser work?

    D. Urban development

    B. 15

  3. What will NOT be given to honor Mr Esser?
