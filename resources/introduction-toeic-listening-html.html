Introduction to TOEICⓇ Listening

This material has been adapted from “La Bible Officielle du Test TOEIC” by Hachette Publishing, ISBN 978-2-01-718380-8

The Listening section within the TOEICⓇ 4-Skills Test has four parts with a total of 45 questions, and lasts approximately 25 minutes:

  • Photographs (3 questions) — Test takers see some photos; for each photo they have to choose the sentence that describes it from the 4 options they hear.
  • Questions - Responses (9 questions) — Test takers hear some questions; each time they have to choose the best response from 3 possible options they hear.
  • Conversations (18 questions : 6 dialogues with 3 questions each) — Test takers listen to some dialogues; there are 3 multiple-choice questions to answer about each dialogue. The questions and the multiple-choice options are shown on the screen.
  • Talks (15 questions : 5 talks with 3 questions each) — Test takers listen to some monologues; there are 3 multiple-choice questions to answer about each talk.

Test takers only listen to each recording ONCE.

The Listening test is adaptive.

This means that the results obtained in the first part of the test determine the difficulty level of the questions in the second part of the test.

Question-Response

Directions: You will hear a question or statement and three responses spoken in English. They will not appear on the screen and will be spoken only one time. Select the best response to each question or statement and click on the letter (A), (B), or (C).

Example

You will hear:

1. Where is the nearest bathroom?

  • (A) Yes, there are two.
  • (B) On your right, opposite the water fountain.
  • (C) They weren't there.

You will answer (B) because the response gives directions. (A) is not possible as it doesn't answer the question “Where?”; (C) is not possible because it is in the past tense and also doesn't answer the question “Where?”

Conversations

Conversations

Directions: You will hear some conversations between two or more people. You will be asked to answer three questions about what the speakers say in each conversation. Select the best response to each question and click on the letter (A), (B), (C), or (D). The conversations will not appear on the screen and will be spoken only one time.

Example

You will hear:

Woman:Hi Daniel, do you have a minute to talk about the client presentation tomorrow?
Man:Sure, Emma. What's the issue?
Woman:I reviewed the slides, and I think we might be missing the updated sales figures for Q2.
Man:Oh, I thought you had already added them. I can update them this afternoon.
Woman:That would be great. Also, the manager asked if we could shorten the presentation by five minutes.
Man:No problem. I'll remove the extra slides on the market overview.
Woman:Perfect. And can you send me the final version before 6 p.m.?
Man:Yes, I'll make sure it's done by then.

1. What is the main topic of the conversation?

  • (A) Scheduling a meeting with a client
  • (B) Ordering office supplies
  • (C) Hiring a new employee
  • (D) Preparing a presentation

2. What problem does Emma mention?

  • (A) The presentation is too short
  • (B) The slides are missing some data
  • (C) The meeting has been cancelled
  • (D) The client is unhappy with the product

3. What will Daniel do next?

  • (A) Contact the client directly
  • (B) Rewrite the entire presentation
  • (C) Update the slides and shorten the presentation
  • (D) Print documents for the meeting

You will answer: D, B, C

Talks

Talks

Directions: You will hear some talks given by a single speaker. You will be asked to answer three questions about what the speaker says in each talk. Select the best response to each question and click on the letter (A), (B), (C), or (D). The talks will not appear on the screen and will be spoken only one time.

Example

You will hear:

Woman:Good morning, everyone. This is a reminder that the IT department will perform a system update tonight starting at 8 p.m. During this time, employees will not be able to access company email or shared files remotely. The update is expected to last approximately three hours. If you are currently working on important documents, please save your files before leaving the office today. Please contact the IT help desk before 5 p.m. if you have any questions. Thank you for your cooperation. In addition, tomorrow morning's training session has been moved from the Horizon Conference Room to the Cedar Conference Room because of maintenance work.

1. What is the purpose of the announcement?

  • (A) To introduce a new employee
  • (B) To share some important updates
  • (C) To invite employees to a company event
  • (D) To explain a new vacation policy

2. What will employees be unable to do tonight?

  • (A) Use the company cafeteria
  • (B) Attend a training session
  • (C) Access their work email at home
  • (D) Enter the office building

3. Why was the training session moved?

  • (A) The instructor is unavailable
  • (B) The room is being used for another meeting
  • (C) The session was cancelled
  • (D) Maintenance work is being done

You will answer: B, C, D
