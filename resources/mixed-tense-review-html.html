Answer the following questions using appropriate tenses.

  • What do you usually do at the weekend?
  • Did you do anything interesting last week?
  • What were you doing at this time yesterday?
  • What's the most interesting book you've ever read?
  • How long have you been studying English?
  • What have you been doing recently to improve your English?
  • What were you like when you were a child?
  • Can you tell me about one of your best childhood memories?

Practice #1: Mixed Tenses (Multiple Choice)

Choose the correct answer (a, b, or c).

  1. I ................ to the gym three times a week.
    a) am going    b) go    c) have gone
  2. She ................ her keys yesterday and couldn't get into her house.
    a) was losing    b) has lost    c) lost
  3. While we ................ lunch, it started to rain.
    a) had    b) were having    c) have had
  4. How long ................ in London?
    a) do you live    b) are you living    c) have you lived
  5. I ................ this book for two hours and I still haven't finished it.
    a) read    b) am reading    c) have been reading
  6. My brother ................ football every Saturday when he was young.
    a) played    b) was playing    c) has played
  7. ................ the new James Bond film yet?
    a) Did you see    b) Have you seen    c) Are you seeing
  8. At 9 o'clock last night, I ................ TV.
    a) watched    b) was watching    c) have watched
  9. We ................ each other since university.
    a) know    b) are knowing    c) have known
  10. She ................ very tired recently because she ................ long hours.
    a) is/works    b) has been/has been working    c) was/worked

Practice #2: Mixed Tenses (Gap Fill)

Fill in the gaps with the correct tense of the verb in brackets.

  1. My neighbour ................ (water) his garden every morning at 6 AM.
  2. Look! The children ................ (build) a snowman in the backyard.
  3. We ................ (miss) the last train because we arrived too late.
  4. While I ................ (cook) dinner, the phone rang three times.
  5. She ................ (never/visit) Australia, but she dreams of going there.
  6. How long ................ you ................ (learn) Spanish? Your pronunciation is excellent!
  7. According to the weather forecast, it ................ (rain) heavily tomorrow.
  8. I think technology ................ (change) our lives dramatically in the next decade.
  9. The museum ................ (close) at 5 PM on weekdays.
  10. Sorry, Mr Khan cannot come to the phone right now - he ................ (attend) an important meeting.
  11. Mozart ................ (compose) over 600 pieces of music during his short life.
  12. The students ................ (take) notes when the fire alarm suddenly went off.
  13. Have you seen my keys anywhere? I ................ (lose) them again!
  14. We ................ (wait) for the bus for over an hour when it finally arrived.
  15. My parents ................ (move) to a smaller house next month because they want to downsize.
  16. My manager says I can go after I have done this. If I ................ (finish) work early, I ................ (go) to the cinema.
  17. If I ................ (be) you, I ................ (accept) the job offer.
  18. Why didn't you tell me about your operation? If I ................ (know) you were in hospital, I ................ (come) to visit you.

Practice #2: Mixed Tenses (Gap Fill) Answers:

  1. waters (Present Simple - habitual action)
  2. are building (Present Continuous - action happening now)
  3. missed (Past Simple - completed action in the past)
  4. was cooking (Past Continuous - action in progress when another action happened)
  5. has never visited (Present Perfect - experience up to now)
  6. have...been learning (Present Perfect Continuous - duration up to now)
  7. will rain / is going to rain (Future - prediction based on evidence)
  8. will change (Future - prediction)
  9. closes (Present Simple - timetable/schedule)
  10. is attending (Present Continuous - action happening now / temporary)
  11. composed (Past Simple - completed action in the past)
  12. were taking (Past Continuous - action in progress when another action happened)
  13. have lost (Present Perfect - recent action with present result)
  14. had been waiting (Past Perfect Continuous - duration up to a point in the past)
  15. are moving / are going to move (Future - planned action)
  16. finish...will go (First conditional - real possibility)
  17. were...would accept (Second conditional - hypothetical situation)
  18. had known...would have come (Third conditional - past hypothetical situation)
