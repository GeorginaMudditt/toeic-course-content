2. Words starting with "h"

ðŸ‘‰ Tip: Light breath at the start of the word.

The /h/ sound is always pronounced in English except if the word has a French origin. If the English language has "borrowed" the word from French, it also borrows the pronunciation - that is, not pronouncing the "h". English "hours" comes from French "heures" so the "h" at the beginning is silent and we use "an" instead of "a".
