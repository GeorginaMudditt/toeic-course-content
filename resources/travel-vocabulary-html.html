Part 4: Discussion Questions

Use the vocabulary from this worksheet to answer the following questions.

  1. Describe a memorable business trip you have taken (or imagine taking). What was your itinerary? Did you experience any problems with your flights or accommodation?
  2. What are the advantages and disadvantages of business travel? Consider factors like jet lag, time zones, expense accounts, and time away from home.
  3. Some companies are reducing business travel to save costs and reduce their carbon footprint. Do you think virtual meetings can replace face-to-face business trips? Why or why not?
  4. What tips would you give to someone taking their first international business trip? Think about check-in procedures, connecting flights, travel insurance, and managing expenses.
