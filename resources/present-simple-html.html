Grammar Point #1 ‚Äì What is the Present Simple?

We use the Present Simple for:

  • daily routines
  • habits
  • things that are always true

Examples:

  • I work in an office.
  • She starts at 9 o'clock.
  • They have lunch at 12.

Grammar Point #3 ‚Äì Negative form

To make a negative sentence:

  • I / You / We / They ‚Üí do not + verb
  • He / She ‚Üí does not + verb
  • (do not = don't, does not = doesn't)

Examples:

  • I don't work on Sunday.
  • She doesn't start at 8.

Grammar Point #4 ‚Äì Questions

For questions:

  • I / You / We / They ‚Üí Do + subject + verb?
  • He / She ‚Üí Does + subject + verb?

Examples:

  • Do you work here?
  • Does she start at 9?
