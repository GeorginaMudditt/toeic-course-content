Theme 2: Conversation

Warm-up

Answer the questions.

  • Do you usually talk to people when you are on holiday?
  • Where do you usually meet people on holiday (hotel, restaurant, tour, etc.)?
  • What do you usually talk about?
  • What difficulties have you experienced when talking to people on holiday?
  • What is the nicest conversation you have had on holiday?

Practise the conversations. Ask some follow-up questions.

Try to use phrases to show interest, such as:

  • "Really?"
  • "How interesting!"
  • "That sounds lovely."
