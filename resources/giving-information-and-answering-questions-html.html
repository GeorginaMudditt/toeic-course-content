Audioscript

Tourist: Excuse me, could you tell me where the Maritime Museum is?

Employee: Of course. Go straight ahead for about two hundred metres and you’ll see it on your right, opposite the old lighthouse.

Tourist: Great. What time does it open?

Employee: It opens at 10 a.m. every day except Monday.

Tourist: And how much is the entrance fee?

Employee: It’s €12 for adults and €6 for children.

Tourist: Is there a guided tour?

Employee: Yes. There’s a guided tour at 11 a.m. and another one at 3 p.m.

Tourist: Perfect. Is there somewhere to eat nearby?

Employee: Yes. There’s a café next to the museum entrance.

Tourist: Can I buy tickets online?

Employee: Yes, you can buy them online or directly at the ticket office.

Tourist: Thank you very much.

Employee: You’re welcome. Enjoy your visit.
