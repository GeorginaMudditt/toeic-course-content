Role Play #1

You are a tourist. Ask in the tourist information centre for some information about this boat trip:

Sunset Boat Cruise

  • 🕒 Departure Time: 7:30 p.m.
  • ⏰ Duration: 2 hours
  • 💵 Price: €35 per adult / €20 per child

  • ✔ Suitable for all ages

Example questions
  • Where does the boat leave from?
  • What time does the cruise start?
  • How long does it last?
  • How much does it cost for two adults?
  • Is the activity suitable for children?
  • Are drinks included?
  • Do we need to book in advance?
