Part 3: Discussion Questions

Use the vocabulary from this worksheet to answer the following questions.

  1. What is your ideal job? Would you prefer to work full-time or part-time? Would you like a permanent position or do freelance work? Explain your reasons.
  2. Describe your career path (real or imagined). What training or qualifications do you need? How long does it take to get a promotion in your field?
  3. What are the advantages and disadvantages of being an entrepreneur compared to working for a company? Think about responsibility, job security, and work-life balance.
  4. Some people commute long distances to work, while others work from home. What do you think is better and why? Do you think working remotely affects career development?
