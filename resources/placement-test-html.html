Speaking

Read Aloud

Please read the text aloud. You will have 45 seconds to prepare. Then you will have 45 seconds to read the text aloud.

If you're constantly checking emails, attending meetings and staring at screens all day, your workspace can feel overwhelming. To escape office fatigue, discover the charming Riverside Café on Forest Street. While relaxing at our screen-free café, you'll enjoy freshly roasted coffee as you savour homemade pastries or healthy lunch options. With its cosy seating, river views and quiet reading nooks, the café is the perfect place for the break you truly deserve. The Riverside Café takes pride in the warm atmosphere it creates for every visitor, allowing you to recharge your batteries before heading back to the office.

Express an opinion

You will give your opinion about a specific topic. Be sure to say as much as you can in the time allowed. You will have 45 seconds to prepare. Then you will have 60 seconds to speak.

Topic: Some people believe it is better to work for a large company, while others prefer working for a small business. What is your opinion about working for a large company versus a small business? Give reasons for your opinion.

Reading

Incomplete Sentences

  1. I couldn't find the report this morning despite ........................ for it everywhere. ( look / looked / looking / to look )
  2. She was offered the job because of her ........................ experience in customer service. ( before / previous / lately / advance )
  3. With the help of one of the IT technicians, the missing account files have been ........................ ( recovers / recovered / recover / recovering )
  4. A private reception for gallery donors will be ........................ on March 5, prior to the grand opening of the exhibition. ( claimed / faced / held / made )
  5. Aurora furnishing is finding it difficult to make a profit in its ........................ competitive market. ( increases / increasingly / increase / increased )
  6. The CEO's explanation was intended to reassure investors, but it did little to ........................ their concerns. ( address / justify / adhere / reinforce )

Reading Comprehension

Dear Mr Martin,

I am writing to confirm the details of the cleaning services for the Halley Valley Rock Festival. As discussed, your company will provide cleaning services throughout the festival grounds from June 14-16.

This year, we have made some changes from previous years. Instead of continuous cleaning, we will need intermittent cleaning services. Additionally, we will provide a temporary office trailer for your staff to use during the festival.

We look forward to working with you again this year.

Matilda Johnson
Festival Coordinator
Halley Valley Foundation

  1. Who most likely is Mr Martin?

    A. A musical performer
    B. A truck driver
    C. A cleaning company representative
    D. A festival coordinator
  2. What is the difference between this year and last year?

    A. The festival lasts longer
    B. The cleaning company will work with the festival company for the first time
    C. People will be camping at the festival
    D. The cleaning company will have a different schedule
  3. According to the email, what will be provided?

    A. Food and water
    B. A sheltered area
    C. Musical equipment
    D. Cleaning supplies

Writing

You will show how well you can write a response to an e-mail. You will have 10 minutes to read and answer the e-mail.

From: Human Resources

To: New Employee

Subject: Welcome to the Team

Sent: March 3, 9:10 A.M.

Welcome to Brightway Solutions! We are excited to have you join our team. Your first day will be next Monday. During your first week, you will participate in orientation and training sessions to help you get familiar with our company and your role.

If you have any questions before your start date, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.

Task: Respond to the e-mail as a new employee. In your response, make at least TWO requests for information related to your first week at work.

Tip: You can also write your response on paper and upload a photo using the file upload option below.
