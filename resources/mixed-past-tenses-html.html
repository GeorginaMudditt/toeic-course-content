Summary

Ji-Soo Park has lived in Barcelona since she started her master's degree there. She is originally from Seoul, where she studied industrial design and worked for an electronics company until 2015. She moved to Spain to study innovation and entrepreneurship, then joined a consulting firm as a product analyst. By 2020 she had been promoted to senior product analyst and was leading research for a new logistics software project.

She then began a part-time PhD in sustainable product design at the Polytechnic University of Catalonia. While she was writing her research proposal, she was still finishing a few consultancy tasks for Nexa. By the time she joined Smartlight in January 2021, she had already completed the first year of her doctorate.

Smartlight makes smart lighting systems for offices and factories. At Smartlight, she has simplified the planning process for new products and has been training junior product managers since last year. Her team has launched three major updates, and a new control dashboard has been adopted by more than 200 site managers. She was promoted to senior product manager in 2024 and now manages a team of nine people, working with colleagues in Spain, Germany, and Poland.

📝 Answers: Profile 2 (gap fill)

  1. started — past simple (finished time: 2018)
  2. has been working — present perfect continuous (from the beginning of 2026 until now)
  3. was promoted — past simple passive (completed event: January 2026)
  4. had been unemployed — past perfect (state before joining Office Inc in 2018)
  5. was preparing / had been preparing — past continuous / past perfect continuous (background activity)
  6. has never worked — present perfect (experience up to now)
  7. made / had made — past simple / past perfect (redundancies in Nov 2017)
  8. completed — past simple (finished time: in 2019)
  9. had already revised — past perfect (action before her return in 2021)
  10. had been coordinating — past perfect continuous (duration before promotion in 2016)
  11. offered — past simple (specific offer after the disciplinary case)
  12. has been supporting — present perfect continuous (ongoing since January 2026)
