What do you notice about the tenses?

Changes When Reporting Speech

Change of pronouns:

"I'm tired. I've been working since six this morning." ‚Üí She said she was tired because she had been working since six that morning.

Backshift of tenses:

  • Present ‚Üí Past
  • Past simple ‚Üí Past perfect
  • Will ‚Üí Would

Time expressions change:

  • today ‚Üí that day
  • tomorrow ‚Üí the next day
  • now ‚Üí then

Speaking #1: Retell Key Moments

Retell some key moments from a book or film using reported speech.

Example 1:

Direct: "I cannot forgive you," Andrew's sister said angrily.

Reported: Andrew's sister insisted angrily that she could not forgive him.

Example 2:

Direct: - "When are you leaving?" Maria asked.
                                  - "I don't know anymore. Everything has changed," John replied.

Reported: Maria asked John when he was leaving. He replied that he didn't know anymore because everything had changed.
