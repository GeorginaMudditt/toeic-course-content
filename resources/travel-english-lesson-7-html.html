Answers

Vocabulary: Verbs

  • to book / to reserve — to arrange something in advance (e.g. a table, a room, a ticket). Book and reserve are often interchangeable.
  • to order — to tell the waiter what food or drink you want.
  • to recommend — to suggest something because you think it is good.
  • to prepare — to make or get food ready (usually done by the chef in the kitchen).
  • to serve — to bring food or drink to the customer at the table.

Vocabulary: Nouns (French translations)

  • starter = entrée
  • main course = plat principal
  • dessert = dessert
  • menu = menu / carte
  • wine list = carte des vins
  • side dish = accompagnement
  • dish = plat
  • (chef’s) special = plat du jour / spécialité du chef
  • recommendation = recommandation
  • allergy = allergie
  • bill = addition / note
  • tip = pourboire
  • service charge = frais de service / service

Vocabulary: Adjectives

Positive (✅): tasty, delicious, creamy, tender, spicy, sweet, fresh

Negative (❌): disgusting, undercooked, overcooked, burnt, bland, too salty
