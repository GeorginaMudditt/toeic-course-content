Part 3: Discussion Questions

Use the vocabulary from this worksheet to answer the following questions.

  1. Think about a time when you received excellent customer service. What made you satisfied with the experience?
  2. Have you ever made a complaint to a company? How did they handle it? Were they able to resolve the issue?
  3. Why is customer feedback important for businesses? How can companies use it to improve customer satisfaction?
  4. What should a company do if it sells a faulty or defective product? Should customers always receive a refund or an exchange?
  5. Why are response time and courteous communication important in customer service?
  6. What can companies do to improve customer retention and encourage clients to stay loyal?
