  2. When was the last time you travelled by plane? Where were you going? Who were you with?
  3. Do you prefer travelling by train or by plane? Why?
  4. Do you usually arrive at the airport or train station early or late?
  5. What do you like doing while you are travelling (reading, listening to music, watching films, sleeping, etc.)?

Audioscripts

Question 1

Hi Tony, we are leaving at 9.00 tomorrow so come to my house on the bus by 8.30. The train leaves at 9.45. My dad is taking us to Oxford station in the car so we don't have to walk there with our bags.

Question 2

Due to technical problems, I'm afraid the one-thirty flight EW268 to Glasgow has been cancelled. The next flight to Glasgow leaves at quarter to nine this evening.

Question 3

Here is the information about the trains arriving on Platform 12. The first train to arrive on platform 12 will be the 2:25 to Exeter Saint Davids. The second train will be the 2:32 to London Paddington. And the third train will be the 2:38 to Cardiff. All trains for Birmingham have been moved to Platform 4.

Question 4

Hello, everyone. Welcome to Greenfield Coach Station. We have three coaches leaving today. The half past ten coach to London. This is leaving shortly. Please get on now. For the quarter past eleven coach to Birmingham, we are expecting a delay of 20 to 30 minutes. The two o'clock coach to Bristol will leave on time.

Question 5

Hi, Sandra. It's Mike. I'm still at the airport waiting for my flight. It was supposed to depart this morning at 9:30, but it got delayed by about two hours due to some kind of problem with the plane. With the new departure time, I won't arrive in Toronto until 1:30 this afternoon. I'll come straight to your office since the meeting starts at 2:00, but I might be a little late. If so, please feel free to start the meeting without me.
