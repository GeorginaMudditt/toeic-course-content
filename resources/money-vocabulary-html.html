Part 3: Discussion Questions

Use the vocabulary from this worksheet to answer the following questions.

  1. What factors should employees consider when comparing salary, bonus, and pension benefits in a job offer?
  2. How can a company balance growth targets with controlling expenses and overheads?
  3. Why might a business choose to offer share options instead of a higher base salary?
  4. What is the difference between fixed costs and variable costs? Give examples from a business you know.
  5. Do you think sales staff should be paid mainly through commission, a fixed salary, or a mix? Why?
  6. What were you taught about managing money at school? What do you think schools should teach about this topic?

  19. Managers questioned whether the hotel bill was a legitimate business expense.
